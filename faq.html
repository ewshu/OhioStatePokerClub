Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ohio State Poker Club?

Ohio State Poker Club is a student organization based at Ohio State, dedicated to fostering a passion for poker among its members.

How can I get involved with Ohio State Poker Club?

To get involved, attend any of our free tournaments, info sessions, or paid cash games. We welcome all Ohio State students to participate in our events and activities. If you're interested in joining the executive board, reach out to Michael at gabe.7@buckeyemail.osu.edu.

How can I join as a general member?

It's really easy! Sign up for our mailing list. Shortly after, you will be let into our Discord.

How can I join the executive committee?

Applications for the executive committee typically happen every fall. To get notified when our applications open, sign up for our mailing list.

How can I become a sponsor?

If you are interested in sponsoring Ohio State Poker Club, we encourage you to get in touch with us at gabe.7@buckeyemail.osu.edu.

How do I sign up for the PokerFlops Tournament?

To participate in our weekly PokerFlops tournaments, follow these steps: Download the Official SoPo App, enter the Club Code: 7485734, and register on the Table. Before you enter the club code, change your name to your full name (ex: Michael Gabe, Michael G). Once you have changed your name and requested, email or Discord message Nathan Edens (Discord: @Nathan Edens, Email: edens.28@buckeyemail.osu.edu) or Richie Saelim (Discord: @Richie Saelim, Email: Saelim.8@buckeyemail.osu.edu) to get approved.
